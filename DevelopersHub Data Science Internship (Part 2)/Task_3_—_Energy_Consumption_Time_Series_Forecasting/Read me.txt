✅ Task 3 — Energy Consumption Time Series Forecasting


Approach:


Parsed and combined Date/Time into a proper datetime index
Resampled over 2 million minute-level readings into hourly averages
Engineered time-based features: hour, day of week, month, weekend flag
Trained and compared ARIMA, Prophet, and XGBoost on the most recent 6 months
Evaluated all three models using MAE and RMSE


Results:

ModelMAERMSEXGBoost0.4960.706Prophet0.6040.747ARIMA0.6690.838

Key Insights:


XGBoost achieved the best performance, showing that well-engineered
time-based features can outperform classical time series models
Prophet was a close second, confirming strong daily and weekly seasonality
ARIMA performed weakest since it only models the series' own past values
without external time-context features
Clear daily consumption pattern: usage peaks in early morning and evening,
consistent with cooking and heating routines


Skills Demonstrated:


Time series forecasting
Feature engineering for temporal data
Model comparison and evaluation (MAE, RMSE)
Temporal data visualization

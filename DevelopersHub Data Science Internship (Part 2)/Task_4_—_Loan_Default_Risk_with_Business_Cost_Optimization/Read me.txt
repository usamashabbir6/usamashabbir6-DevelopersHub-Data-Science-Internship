✅ Task 4 — Loan Default Risk with Business Cost Optimization


Approach:


Selected 15 key features from 120+ available columns
Cleaned DAYS_BIRTH/DAYS_EMPLOYED into interpretable Age and Years Employed
Trained Logistic Regression and CatBoost, compared using AUC
Defined business costs: False Negative (missed default) = $10,000,
False Positive (wrongly rejected customer) = $500
Tested thresholds from 0.1–0.9 to find the cost-minimizing decision point

accuracy — it's the one that minimizes real financial impact
